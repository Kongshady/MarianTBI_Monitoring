# MARIAN Technology Bussiness Incubator(TBI) Project Management Information System

The Marian Technology Business Incubator (TBI) aimed at fostering the growth and development of startups by providing them with support in technical, operational, and strategic areas. The selection of this organization as the subject of analysis is based on its unique role in the incubation ecosystem and its growing need for more efficient and effective ICT solutions, especially in tracking progress and managing incubatee needs.
